# GM-Only Plot Information

## Secret Plot

The cult leader operating in Throal is actually Councilor Verak, the elder of
the merchant quarter. He has been secretly supplying the Theran Empire with
kaer locations in exchange for personal protection during the coming Scourge.

This is GM-only information. Players must not learn this until the climactic
reveal in Session 10.

## Hidden NPC Motivations

Seraphina the herbalist is a Nethermancer spy. Her healing herbs are laced with
a mild truth serum that makes customers slightly more forthcoming about
campaign-related secrets.
